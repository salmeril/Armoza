    Hypothyroidism

    It is characterized by circulating concentrations below the normal level of thyroid hormones, it causes a constellation of symptoms characterized by the slowing of physical and mental functions.
    The most important clinical aspects of hypothyroidism are decreased mental, cardiovascular and neuromuscular activities.

    • Ronca voice
    • Slow speech (Bradilalia)
    • Letargo
    • Cold intolerance
    • Memory alterations
    • Constipation or constipation

    • Fatigue

    • Facial swelling

    • Somnolence
    • Fertility problems
    • Decreased libido

    • Irregular menstruation, amenorrhea
    • Inexplicable weight gain
