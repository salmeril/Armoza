    Sexually transmitted diseases

    Sexually transmitted diseases (STDs) also called venereal diseases, are infectious-contagious diseases that are transmitted through sexual intercourse, are serious, sometimes painful and can cause a lot of damage.Some may infect sexual and reproductive organs.
    Others (HIV and hepatitis b) cause general infections in the body.
    Sometimes you can have one of these diseases even if you do not present any symptoms.On other occasions, symptoms may disappear for themselves, but the disease is not cured if treatment is not received.
    It is necessary to remember that social contact, and not only sexual can allow the dissemination of these diseases; since they are not only transmitted through the vagina or by the anus, but also by oral sex, so also through infected or blood transfusion, the latter in the case of hepatitis and AIDS.
    Although some STDs are incurable, most can be cured.
