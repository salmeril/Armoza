    Allergic reactions also called hypersensitivity reaction, it is an inappropriate response of the immune system to a substance that is not normally harmful. The immune system is a highly complex defense mechanism, which helps us fight infections, this is identifying strange invaders and mobilizing white blood cells to combat them.In some people, the immune system erroneously identifies a non-toxic substance as an invader, and white cells overact and do more damage to the body than the invader. So, the allergic response itself becomes a disease
